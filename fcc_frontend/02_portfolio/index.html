About Me

I'm a Geologist and a Chemist by academic background, and I've been working in the oil industry for a little under 6 years. I've recently left (voluntarily) to pursue a career change and a location change.

I love solving puzzles, and I love working with large, complex datasets to come up with solutions... particularly when there are no easy answers!

I also love computers, and I've always had a knack for programming. This webpage was created as part of a project for FreeCodeCamp, but it's a great opportunity to showcase my progress towards my Full Stack Web Development Certification.

If you'd like to know more about me, please check out my LinkedIn page, where you can find an up-to-date snapshot of my resume and my experiences.
